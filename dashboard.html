Operational Memory & Investigation

Operational activity is persistently recorded as structured event data. This creates a verifiable operational record that can be reviewed, reconstructed, and documented without relying on raw video playback. All investigation and reporting functions operate directly on this event record.

Because nothing is discarded once the moment passes, incidents can be replayed as factual timelines across cameras, zones, and time ranges. The result is not just live awareness, but a durable, queryable record of how the site actually operated.
