What changes when you add a site intelligence layer

Most systems record video or push alerts. Neither creates usable knowledge. An intelligence layer changes what information you retain and what you can do with it.

Continuous observation of site activity

Activity is observed continuously without relying on someone watching. Presence, movement, and behaviour are detected automatically across the site.

Consistent analysis applied across the site

Specialised modules apply consistent rules to every observation. Safety, access, and behaviour are evaluated the same way, every time — without fatigue or variation.

Activity converted into structured, time-stamped records

Observations become structured events — factual, normalised, and auditable. These are not alerts or clips. They are records of what occurred.

Persistent site memory rather than momentary visibility

All events are stored persistently. Nothing is lost by default. Activity from last week or last month can be reviewed with the same fidelity as yesterday.

Clear reporting and review based on recorded facts

Shift handovers, compliance records, incident reports, and audit trails are generated directly from stored events. Documentation is consistent and traceable.
