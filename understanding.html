Understanding what matters on site

The platform observes activity, recognises context, and records what is relevant — without constant human monitoring.

Most camera systems either record video or trigger basic alerts. On their own, they provide limited understanding of what is actually happening. Activity passes by, alerts create noise, and little usable information remains.

This platform converts what cameras see into structured, time-stamped records. It applies consistent rules across feeds, stores observations as factual events, and makes site activity queryable — both in real time and after the fact.

Safety and protective equipment

The platform observes PPE usage across camera feeds and logs compliance automatically. Safety conditions are monitored consistently and recorded for later review or audit.

Vehicles and movement

Vehicle and pedestrian activity across yards, entrances, and operational areas is tracked over time. Movement patterns become queryable data.

Sensitive or restricted zones

Activity in defined areas is recorded with full context. When something happens, the system captures a structured timeline for review and reporting.

Noteworthy events

Unusual or important moments are preserved as factual, time-stamped records. Events are structured, stored, and ready for investigation or handover.

The platform does not replace people. It gives teams clarity, context, and memory across complex environments.
