Structured intelligence

Context Engine applies AI-driven computer vision across your existing CCTV network to continuously analyse activity on site. Instead of generating raw footage or basic motion alerts, our intelligence layer transforms detections into structured events and insights. These events form a persistent operational record; enabling search, investigation, compliance reporting, and real-time oversight across your physical environment.

Core capabilities

  • • Continuous analysis across all monitored areas
  • • Rule enforcement across cameras and zones
  • • Structured event creation (not clips or alerts)
  • • Persistent site record for insights, review, and reporting

Computer vision modules

Specialised computer vision modules apply defined rules to structured activity, enabling site-specific oversight such as:

Safety & PPE compliance

Automatically detect required safety equipment and log non-compliance.

Vehicle & pedestrian movement

Track site access and movement patterns across zones.

Restricted zones & access control

Monitor defined areas and record unauthorised access.

Incident investigation & review

Search structured event records instead of scrubbing raw footage.

How it works

From observation to site intelligence.

Observe

Live CCTV and camera feeds are continuously analysed on site, capturing presence, movement, and activity across all monitored areas.

Analyse

Computer vision modules apply consistent rules to each feed, assessing safety conditions, access behaviour, and site-specific activity patterns.

Structure

Relevant observations are converted into structured, time-stamped events that describe what happened, where, and when — not alerts or raw clips.

Store

All events are stored as a persistent site record, creating a factual history of activity that can be revisited at any time.

Review

Search, query, and generate reports from this record to support investigations, handovers, compliance, and oversight.

Built for offline

All analysis and processing runs locally so your data never leaves your environment, making our intelligence layer suitable for regulated industries, sensitive, or restricted sites.

Key principles

  • All processing runs locally on-site
  • No mandatory cloud dependency
  • No requirement for external AI services
  • Full control over data retention, access, and storage
