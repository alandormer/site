Dashboard

The dashboard is where operators work with the structured event data generated by the platform.

All features here operate on the stored event record — not raw video playback. This allows teams to review activity, generate reports, and investigate incidents from factual, time-stamped data.

Review and timelines

Understand what happened, when it happened, and in what sequence.

Event timelines

View activity as a chronological sequence of events. Filter by camera, zone, event type, or time range. Each event includes timestamp, location, and classification.

Incident review

When something happens, review the sequence of events leading up to and following the incident. Context is preserved in the structured record.

Activity summaries

See aggregated views of site activity by hour, shift, or day. Identify patterns and anomalies without manual review.

Reports and handovers

Generate structured outputs from the event record in multiple formats.

Shift handover reports

Automatically generated summaries of activity during a shift. Outgoing teams can hand over a factual record to incoming teams.

Incident reports

Structured reports for specific incidents including timeline, events, and supporting evidence. Suitable for internal review or external submission.

Compliance and audit records

Generate records demonstrating safety compliance, access control adherence, or operational procedures over defined periods.

Export formats

Reports can be exported as HTML, PDF, or CSV depending on the use case and recipient requirements.

Queries and investigations

Search the event record to answer specific questions about past activity.

Search by criteria

Query events by time range, location, event type, or specific conditions. Find what you need without reviewing hours of footage.

Cross-camera queries

Search across all cameras and zones simultaneously. Track activity as it moves through different areas of the site.

Historical investigation

Investigate patterns or incidents from days, weeks, or months ago. The structured record retains full detail over time.

All dashboard features operate on structured event data. This means reports, queries, and timelines are generated from factual records — not by scrubbing through raw video. The result is faster review, consistent outputs, and auditable documentation.
