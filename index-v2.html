The problem with most camera systems

Most CCTV systems either record video continuously or trigger basic motion alerts. Neither approach creates usable knowledge about what actually happened on site.

Video recordings require manual review. Alert systems generate noise and interruptions. When something happens, teams are left piecing together fragments of footage with no structured record of events.

What the platform does

Observes activity across camera feeds

Computer vision models analyse live CCTV feeds continuously in the background. Presence, movement, and behaviour are detected automatically without requiring someone to watch.

Applies consistent analysis rules

Configurable modules evaluate safety compliance, access control, movement patterns, and behaviour. The same rules are applied consistently across all feeds, without fatigue or variation.

Converts observations into structured events

Activity is converted into factual, time-stamped records. These are not alerts or video clips — they are structured data that can be queried, filtered, and reported on.

Stores events as an operational record

All events are stored persistently. Activity from last week or last month can be reviewed with the same detail as today. Nothing is lost once the moment passes.

Example use cases

The platform applies to any environment with cameras where understanding activity matters more than just recording it.

Safety and PPE monitoring

Detect and log PPE compliance, unsafe behaviour, and safety incidents as they occur. Build a record over time for review and audit.

Vehicle and pedestrian movement

Track movement across yards, gates, and operational areas. Understand flow patterns and identify anomalies.

Restricted zones and access control

Monitor entry to sensitive areas. Record who accessed what, when, and for how long — without manual logging.

Incident review and investigation

When something happens, query the event record to understand context, timeline, and contributing factors. No need to scrub through hours of footage.

How it works

Five stages turn raw camera feeds into structured operational data.

1

Observe

Camera feeds are analysed continuously using computer vision.

2

Analyse

AI models detect people, vehicles, behaviour, and conditions.

3

Structure

Observations become time-stamped, factual event records.

4

Store

Events are stored persistently for later retrieval.

5

Review

Query, report, and investigate from the structured record.

The platform runs entirely on-site. All processing happens locally. Video and data do not leave your environment unless you explicitly export them.

  • • AI models run locally — no data sent to third-party services
  • • Works in air-gapped, offline, or network-restricted environments
  • • Full control over data retention, access, and deletion
